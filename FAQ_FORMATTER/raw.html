Using Taulia Q&A

Q. What is Taulia?

  • A. Taulia is a partner platform that allows SAP suppliers to view the status of their invoices, receive remittance details, and request early payment for approved invoices. All accounts payable processes will continue to be owned by SAP.

Q. Who should I invite to my portal account?

  • A. Please add others to your company who will need access to the portal including for checking invoices, payments, etc. Click here to learn how to add users to your account.

 

Please be informed that the maintenance of your Taulia account is fully under your responsibility.

Make sure that in the event of any change (users leaving your company, access removal, new admins, split/merge of companies, etc) your designated Taulia account administrator manages the complete access in the User Manager, by activating or deactivating users appropriately.

If you have only one admin user on the account, please be sure to add a new admin user before you leave the company. This will ensure that your company does not lose access to your Taulia portal.

Invoicing Q&A

Click here to learn how to view your invoice status and details.

Use the SAP supplier platform to monitor the status of your invoices and request early payment.

 

Q. Is the process for submitting my invoice to SAP changing?

  • A. No. Please continue to submit your invoices to SAP like you do today. Your invoice will appear in the Taulia platform once SAP enters your invoice in their accounting system. They may have an "Approved", "In Process", "Rejected/Voided"," or "Paid" status.

Q. I have questions related to the details of my invoice. Who should I contact?

  • A. Please contact SAP directly. Invoices that appear in the platform are direct reflections of the details entered by SAP.

Q. How soon will I see my invoice in the portal after I submit them to SAP?

  • A. Invoices will appear in the portal once they have been matched against receipts by SAP. They may have an "Approved", "In Process", "Rejected/Voided"," or "Paid" status. Click here to learn what each invoice status mean.

Q. What if my invoice is voided/rejected, what should I do?

  • A. Please contact SAP directly. Do not contact Taulia Support. Taulia does not approve/reject invoices.

Q. I can't find the invoice I submitted to SAP. What should I do?

  • A. Please contact SAP directly if you are unable to find an invoice you submitted for processing.

Early Payment & Discounting Q&A

Use the Taulia platform to accelerate the payment of your invoices through the early payment options.

Q. I have questions regarding the early payment rates for invoices. Who should I contact?

  • A. Please contact Taulia Support for any early payment related questions.

Q. Why do some approved invoices do not have an early payment offer?

  • A. Not every approved invoice will have an early payment offer. It is within SAP discretion to offer early payment on any approved invoice. Please contact SAP if you have question.

Q. My invoice is approved but I can't request/accept early payment. Why?

  • Your company is not part of SAP early payment program. (contact SAP to confirm)
  • If you want to request early payment for the first time and you have multiple bank accounts registered with SAP that is showing in the My Details > Bank Accounts section of the portal. You must contact Taulia Support first and inform them that you are not able to see early payment offers due to having multiple bank accounts. Let them know which bank account you would like to use to receive early payment. Once the bank account for early payment is specificied, you will start seeing the early payment offers for your approved invoices.
  • The current date is too close to the invoice due date.
  • Due to credit notes in your account.

Q. I have questions regarding the early payment rates for invoices. Who should I contact?

  • A. Please contact Taulia Support for any early payment related questions.

The Taulia solution offers a Cashflow Functionality option that will automatically request early payment as soon as your invoice is approved so that you never miss the early payment window.
